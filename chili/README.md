# Chili

## Ingredients

* 2 lb ground beef
* 1 large onion
* 1 - 1 1/2 can tomato sauce
* 2-3 cups dried beans
* 2 jalapeno peppers
* 2 bell peppers (any color)
* 4-5 stalks celery
* Corn
* Diced tomatoes
* 4-6 minced garlic cloves
* 1/2 can adobo pepper
* Cilantro
* 1 cup chicken stock
* Cumin
* Lime juice
* Yogurt
* Shredded cheddar
* Chili powder
* Salt
* Pepper
* Thyme (optional)
* Rosemary (optional)

### Vegetarian Ingredients

#### Omit:
* ground beef
* chicken stock

#### Add:
* Mushrooms
* Diced potatoes
* Vegetable stock

#### Omit if vegan:
* Yogurt
* Shredded cheese

## Directions

### Beans

1. Add dried beans to instapot. Fill the rest of the pot with water.
1. Set instapot setting to "Beans" on High setting and cook for 30 minutes
1. Drain liquid and set aside beans for chili

### Chili

1. Dice all vegetables. Leave onion separate from the rest.
1. Using a big soup pot on a stove, sweat onions for about 5 minutes.
1. Add beef and rosemary and thyme and salt and pepper, and brown until beef is cooked.
1. Add chicken stock, tomato sauce, and the rest of the vegetables to the pot.
1. Add beans to the pot. You may have leftover beans if there's too much.
1. Add chili powder and cumin to taste. You may need as much as 1/4 cup of chili powder.
1. Add more tomato sauce if needed.
1. Add cilantro to pot. Use stems for cooking, save leaves for serving.
1. Bring chili to boil
1. Set stove to LOW heat and cover partially. Simmer for 1-2 hours or until ready to serve
1. Serve with cheddar, yogurt, lime, and cilantro.
