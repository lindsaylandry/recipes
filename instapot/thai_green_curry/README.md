# Thai Green Curry

## Ingredients

* 3 tablespoon Thai Green curry paste preferably Maesri or Mae Ploy
* 13.5 oz. coconut milk (1 can)
* 1/2 bunch cilantro
* ½ teaspoon cumin powder
* 3 lb chicken thighs
* ¼ cup low sodium chicken broth or water
* 2 Tbsp fish sauce more to taste
* 1 Tbsp shrimp paste or more to taste
* 1 lime
* 1/2 onion
* 4 cloves garlic
* 1/4 cup ginger (maybe too much)
* 4 lime leaves slightly bruised 
* ¼ cup Thai Basil leaves
* Vegetable Add-Ins
 * 1 cup green bell pepper cubed or strips
 * 1 cup zucchini sliced or strips
 * 1 cup potatoes sliced
 * 1 cup broccoli
 * 1 cup mushrooms

## Directions

1. Blend together onions, ginger, garlic
1. To instant pot, add chicken broth, chicken, potatoes, spices, cilantro, fish sauce, shrimp paste, broccoli, mushrooms, onion,  curry paste
1. Close Instant Pot and pressure cook on High Pressure for 30 minutes to 1 hour
1. Release pressure
1. Add in coconut milk, lime juice, bell pepper, zucchini, lime leaves.
1. Select Saute and cook until vegetables are crisp-tender, 3 to 5 minutes.
1. Taste and adjust with more fish sauce, shrimp paste or lime juice.
1. Stir in the Thai basil leaves.
1. Serve with rice.
