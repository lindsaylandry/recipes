# Matcha White Chocolate Cookies

## Ingredients

* 3/4 cup unsalted butter, room temperature
* 1/2 cup brown sugar
* 3/4 cup granulated sugar
* 1 large egg
* 1 tsp vanilla extract
* 1 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
* 1 tsp baking soda
* pinch of salt
* 2 tbsp matcha powder
* 1/2 cup white chocolate, roughly chopped (6.3oz)

## Directions

1. In a large mixing bowl whisk together room temperature butter, sugar, and brown sugar with electric mixer until light and fluffy
1. Add egg and vanilla extract and mix for 2 minutes
1. Add plain flour, baking soda, salt and matcha powder into wet mixture and stir until just combined
1. Fold in the chopped white chocolate
1. Cover bowl with cling wrap and place in the fridge to chill for 1 hour
1. Line baking sheet with parchment paper
1. Preheat oven to 180C/355F
1. Divide chilled cookie dough into 12 balls and place on lined tray
1. Bake for 12 minutes, or until lightly golden
1. Remove and cool on cooling rack
