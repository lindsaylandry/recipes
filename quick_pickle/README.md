# Quick Pickle

## Ingredients

* 1 1/2 cup water
* 1 1/2 cup vinegar
* 3 tbsp sugar
* 1-1/2 tbsp salt
* 2 tbsp dill
* 15 peppercorns
* Chopped veg/fruit for pickling
* 2-3 coarsely sliced garlic cloves (optional)

## Directions

1. Combine water, vinegar, sugar, and salt in saucepan and bring to boil
1. Put veg, garlic, dill, and peppercorns in a large jar
1. Remove pickling liquid from heat and pour into jar until filled
1. Leave partially covered on counter to cool
1. Once cooled, cover with lid and refrigerate
