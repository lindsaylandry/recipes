# Cilantro Lime Avocado Sauce

## Ingredients
* 2 ripe avocados
* 2 limes
* 1 bunch fresh cilantro
* 1 green pepper (jalapeno/poblano/etc)
* sea salt
* ¼ cup water

## Instructions
1. Halve avocados take out pits and scoop flesh into blender
1. Add in lime juice and cilantro.
1. Remove stem, seeds and membranes from pepper and roughly chop, adding to bowl of food processor.
1. Sprinkle in the salt.
1. Process until everything is thoroughly mixed.
1. Pour in ¼ cup water and process until smooth. 
1. Taste for seasoning, adding more salt or lime juice as needed. For a thinner dressing, add 1 Tablespoon water at a time until desired thickness.
1. Store in glass jar with tight fitting lid in fridge up to a week. Mix well before serving.
