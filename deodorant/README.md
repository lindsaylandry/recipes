# Deodorant

## Ingredients

* 3 tbsp coconut oil
* 3 tbsp unrefined shea butter
* 1/3 cup arrowroot powder
* 2 tbsp baking soda
* Tea tree oil (optional)
* Essential oil for fragrance (optional)

## Directions

1. Using a saucepan, add 1-2 inches of water to bottom.
1. Place a jar in the middle of the saucepan
1. Set on stove on low-medium heat
1. Add all ingredients except oil, and mix with a spoon as ingredients melt
1. Using oven-safe gloves, pour jar of ingredients into small glass jars.
1. Add 5-10 drops of tea tree oil to each jar
1. Add 10-20 drops of essential oil to each jar
1. Using toothpick, stir to incorporate oils
1. Set jars out to dry

### If deodorants are too liquidy

1. Put jar in microwave for 20 seconds
1. Add equal parts arrowroot powder and baking soda until desired consistency is achieved

## Notes

* deodorant comes out liquidy. I adjusted these amounts based on the last batch, may need to update again.
