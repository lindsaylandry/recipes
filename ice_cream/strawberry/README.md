# Strawberry Ice Cream

## Ingredients: Ice Cream Base
* 1 1/2 cups whole milk
* 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
* 1/4 cup dried strawberry powder(?)
* 1/8 tsp salt
* 3/4 cup sugar
* 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

## Ingredients: Macerated Strawberries
* 1 cup strawberries
* 2 tbsp granulated sugar
* Half lemon

## Directions
### Strawberries
1. Chop strawberries and add to a bowl
1. Pour sugar and lemon over strawberries and mix
1. Cover and refrigerate overnight

### Ice Cream
1. Pour milk, salt, vanilla, and sugar into saucepan and bring to a gentle boil, then remove heat

1. Add paste to milk mixture and combine until smooth
1. Churn in ice cream maker for 15 minutes
1. Add macerated strawberries and mix for another 5-10 minutes
1. For firmer ice cream, transfer to airtight container and freeze for 2 to 4 hours before serving.
1. Store in an airtight container in the freezer for up to a month
